About us

We take pride in creating the perfect user experience and a high quality, maintainable codebase. (We even write documentation!)

We are a small team of motivated technologists taking on projects that both challenge us personally and are meaningful to the world. We specialize in Mobile Application (both Hybrid and Native), progressive web applications and full-stack IT solutions. We like working with anything from Digital Ocean and AWS to the Raspberry Pi.
